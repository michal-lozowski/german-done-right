du musst - you have to / you need to (informal tone)
er muss - he has to / he needs to
sie muss - she has to / she needs to
wir müssen - we have to / we need to
ihr müsst - you guys have to / you guys need to
sie müssen - they have to / they need to
Sie müssen - you have to / you need to (formal tone)
man muss - one has to
